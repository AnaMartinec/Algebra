Scones: the most popular resplendent of snacks

Occasionally maligned and misunderstood; the scone is a quintessentially British classic

Recipe and serving suggestions follows

Ingredients

  • 350g self-raising flour, and a little spare for dusting
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 85g butter
  • 3 tbsp caster sugar
  • 175ml milk
  • 1tsp vanilla extract
  • sqeeze lemon juice
  • beaten egg, to glaze
  • jam and clotted cream to serve
