Paris

Paris (English: /ˈpærɪs/; French pronunciation: ​[paʁi] (listen)) is the capital and most populous city of France, with an estimated
population of 2,165,423 residents in 2019 in an area of more than 105 km² (41 sq mi),[5] making it the fourth-most populated city in
the European Union as well as the 30th most densely populated city in the world in 2022.
